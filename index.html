Carubbi's Highlighter.js

Supercharge Your Web Animations

Highlighter.js is a powerful tool for creating stunning animated effects on your web elements. With its simple and intuitive API, create smooth transitions and eye-catching visual effects effortlessly.

Demo: Syntax Highlighting

                    
await highlight(
    target,
    [
        {duration:2, backgroundColor:'red'},
        {duration:1, backgroundColor:'orange', inTime:1},
        {duration:.5, backgroundColor:'green', inTime:99}
    ]);
